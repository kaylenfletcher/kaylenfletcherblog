About Me

A career devoted to little ones

Kaylen, OTR/L, graduated from the University of Tennessee Health Science Center in 2015 with a Master's degree in Occupational Therapy.

She has devoted her career to the pediatric population across a wide variety of settings — from early intervention and home health to schools and outpatient clinics — supporting a diverse range of diagnoses and ages.

Kaylen is a DIR-Proficient clinician with a deep passion for play-based therapy and the belief that every child deserves to thrive in their own way. Outside the clinic, you'll find her crafting macramé, getting lost in a good book, or wrangling her beloved weenie dogs.

Credentials & Approach

Grounded in DIRFloortime®

The DIRFloortime® model is a relationship-based, play-centered framework for understanding and supporting children's development. Kaylen holds the highest level of DIRFloortime® certification — the Certificate of Proficiency — and brings this deep expertise to every child and family she works with.
